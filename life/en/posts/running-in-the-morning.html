The Beginning of a New Routine

Two weeks ago, I planned to run with my colleagues before work.
We agreed to meet on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays at 8 AM, run, shower, and then head to work.

The distance is 4km, and we agreed to run at the pace of the member with the lowest ability.
Penalty for absence: 5,000 won.

After Two Weeks

About two weeks have passed, and we're all participating diligently.
At first, everyone struggled, but now we run comfortably and even chat while running.

Everyone's getting older, so there were cases of joint pain along the way.
But everyone runs well now.
We've all shed our chubbiness.

Gratitude for Colleagues

Office work is often boring.
Without colleagues, going to work would be quite difficult.
I'll pause to capture this simple gratitude.
