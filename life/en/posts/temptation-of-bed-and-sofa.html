A Familiar Morning

Waking up early in the morning isn't difficult anymore.
My body has adapted.

A Productive Morning

This morning I woke up and did some development work.
I made the blog's series UI look cooler.

As usual, I heated up a bagel and brewed tea.
It's completely become a routine now.

Yesterday's Realization

I realized something yesterday.
If I just stay home on weekends, I don't use my time efficiently.

When I'm at home, the bed and sofa call to me.
I can't resist that temptation.
Before I know it, I find myself lying down.

A Resolution

I need to go outside on weekends.
A café, a library, anywhere is fine.
As long as it's a place without a bed and sofa.
