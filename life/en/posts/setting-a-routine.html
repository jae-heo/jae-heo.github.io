I felt like there wasn't enough time in a day.
Every time I felt this way, I ended up going to bed later.

Looking at the results, it was actually less efficient.
I felt both physical and mental strain.

So I pondered about it.
It seemed like my ambitions were greater than my capabilities.

How can I improve time efficiency?
I decided to start with small changes.

I decided to fix my wake-up and sleep schedule.
Wake up at 6 AM, go to bed at 10 PM.
