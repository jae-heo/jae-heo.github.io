The Freshness of Saturday Morning

Today is Saturday.
Waking up early on weekends feels refreshing.

Busy Daily Life

My life is quite busy. During weekdays, there's no time to rest. Time flies by in an instant.
When I go to the office, there's a mountain of work.
Even a brief thought reveals endless tasks to do.

Time Gifted by Routine

The routine provides one rest period in my busy life. It's the morning time.
When I wake up at 6 AM, no one disturbs me.
It's entirely my own time.

A Leisurely Morning

I leisurely prepare breakfast.
Heat up a bagel, boil an egg. Steep the rooibos tea bags I bought in bulk.
While eating, I briefly ponder what to do before heading to work.
Should I read a book? Study English? Write a blog?

Enjoying This Moment

I don't know how long this routine will remain valid.
So until then, let's fully enjoy it.
