Today I woke up at 6:30 AM.
Just 4 days into my routine, I overslept by 30 minutes.

Actually, there's a reason for this.
Last night after work, I went to see my girlfriend for a bit.

We had dinner together and studied at a nearby café.
Since my routine is to sleep at 10 PM, I planned to get home by 9 PM.

But I couldn't bring myself to leave. I felt reluctant somehow.
So I went home at 9:15 PM.

I went to bed a bit late. Fell asleep at 10:20 PM.
