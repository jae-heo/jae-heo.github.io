Today is Saturday.
Waking up early on weekends feels refreshing.

My life is quite busy.
During weekdays, there's no time to rest. Time flies by in an instant.
When I go to the office, there's a mountain of work. Even a brief thought reveals mountains of work.

The routine provides one rest period in my busy life.
It's the morning time.

When I wake up at 6 AM, no one disturbs me.

I leisurely prepare breakfast.
Heat up a bagel, boil an egg.
Steep the rooibos tea bags I bought in bulk.

While eating, I briefly ponder what to do before heading to work.
Should I read a book? Study English? Write a blog?

I don't know how long this routine will remain valid.
Until then, let's fully enjoy it.
